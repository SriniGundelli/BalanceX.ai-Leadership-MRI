# About BalanceX.ai

BalanceX.ai is a practitioner-led, intelligence-driven executive search and leadership advisory firm focused on helping founders, CEOs, and boards make smarter hiring decisions.

---

## Our Approach

**We believe executive hiring is broken at the front end.**

Most hiring mistakes don't happen during the search — they happen before it starts. Vague role definitions. Unrealistic salary bands. Misunderstood talent pools. Hidden misalignment.

Rather than jumping straight into a full search, we run a diagnostic first: an MRI of the role and market that gives you clarity before you commit time, budget, or attention.

**Then, if you want to move forward, we help you find the right person.**

## What We Do

### The Leadership MRI Scan
A same-day intelligence diagnostic for executive hiring. Clarity before you commit to a full search.

**Includes:**
- Role clarity memo (success profile, non-negotiables, background range)
- Market map (where talent sits, how to reach them, realistic timelines)
- Compensation analysis (market rates, positioning, offer strategy)
- Risk assessment (where this search will be hard, and why)

### Full Retained Search
If you want us to find the person, we take the lead.

**Includes:**
- Sourcing (direct outreach, network, recruiters)
- Initial screening and qualification
- Candidate briefing and coaching
- Interview coordination and feedback

### Advisory & Coaching
You're running the search. We're in the room helping.

**Includes:**
- Candidate evaluation and feedback
- Offer strategy and negotiation coaching
- Onboarding plan and 90-day integration
- Ongoing check-ins and troubleshooting
