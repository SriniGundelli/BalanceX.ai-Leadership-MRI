# Process & Timeline

## The MRI Process (Same-Day Delivery)

### Step 1: Briefing Call (20-30 minutes)

**What happens:**
- We discuss the role, business context, and success criteria
- You share constraints (budget, timeline, culture)
- We explore what "good" looks like in your company
- You ask questions about the MRI process

**What we need from you:**
- ✅ Role title and reporting line
- ✅ Key accountabilities (top 3-5)
- ✅ Success metrics (how you'll know this hire is working)
- ✅ Hiring timeline and constraints
- ✅ Any previous hiring attempts and lessons learned

**Scheduling:** Book [directly on Cal.com](https://cal.com/BalanceX.ai/20min)

---

### Step 2: Intelligence Phase (4-6 hours, same afternoon)

**What happens:**
We run a comprehensive market mapping using:

- 🤖 **Claude + AI-enabled research** — Real-time market analysis, news, and signal processing
- 📊 **Proprietary databases** — Executive movement, compensation benchmarks, talent flow
- 🔗 **Network intelligence** — Industry intel, recruiter insights, market pattern analysis
- 📈 **Historical data** — Hiring trends for this role type, typical time-to-hire, success rates

**What we research:**
1. **Role benchmarking** — How other companies define and position this role
2. **Talent pool mapping** — Where credible candidates sit, their backgrounds, motivations
3. **Competitive landscape** — Companies hiring for similar roles, potential poaching targets
4. **Compensation analysis** — Market rates, equity norms, total comp positioning
5. **Sourcing strategy** — Best channels to reach qualified candidates
6. **Risk assessment** — Where this search will be hard, and why

**You don't need to do anything during this phase** — we're in research mode.

---

### Step 3: Read-Out Call (20-30 minutes)

**What happens:**
- We walk through findings and recommendations
- You ask questions and explore implications
- We discuss next steps (full search, different approach, etc.)
- We discuss timeline and process recommendations

**What you'll have:**
- 🎯 Role clarity memo (success profile, non-negotiables, background range)
- 🗺️ Talent map (where candidates sit, how to reach them, shortlist shape)
- 💰 Compensation summary (base, bonus, equity expectations)
- 🚩 Risk assessment (hard spots, mitigation strategies)
- 📋 Recommended hiring process and timeline

**How we deliver:**
- Written findings (3-5 pages) + talking points
- Visual talent map (if helpful)
- Follow-up email with key takeaways

## What Happens After

After the MRI, you have options:

### Option 1: Run the Search Yourself
You now have:
- Clear role definition
- Talent map and sourcing channels
- Compensation positioning
- Risk intelligence

Use it to brief an internal recruiter or hiring manager. We can advise on sourcing strategy if helpful.

### Option 2: Full Retained Search
We take the lead finding and vetting candidates:
- Sourcing (direct outreach, network, recruiters)
- Initial screening and qualification
- Candidate briefing and coaching
- Interview coordination and feedback

**Timeline:** 45-90 days depending on market difficulty  
**Pricing:** Usually 20% of first-year salary (negotiable)

### Option 3: Advisory + Your Team
You run the search with your internal team. We advise:
- Candidate evaluation and feedback
- Offer strategy and negotiation
- Onboarding plan
- Ongoing check-ins (first 90 days)

## FAQ: Process

**Q: What if I can't do the briefing call the same day?**  
A: No problem. We can schedule briefing for when it works for you. Research phase starts after the call.

**Q: Can we do this for multiple roles?**  
A: Yes. We can batch research or run them sequentially. Batch pricing available.

**Q: What if we need the findings in a specific format?**  
A: Let us know during the briefing. We can create executive summary, full memo, presentation deck, or custom format.

**Q: How do I know if the MRI is worth the investment?**  
A: Good questions to ask yourself:
- Is this role mission-critical to my business?
- Have we struggled to hire for roles like this before?
- Would $2,500-5,000 of clarity now save me 6 months of search time?
- Do I have internal misalignment on what this role should be?

If you answered "yes" to 2+ of those, the MRI is probably worth it.

**Q: What if the findings suggest we shouldn't hire?**  
A: That's valuable. Sometimes the market intelligence shows that the role is unfillable, the price is unjustifiable, or timing is wrong. If that's the case, we'll tell you — and you'll have saved months by learning it now rather than discovering it during a failed search.
