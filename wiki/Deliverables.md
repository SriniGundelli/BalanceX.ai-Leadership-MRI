# Deliverables

When your Leadership MRI Scan is complete, you receive four core deliverables.

---

## 1. Role Clarity & Success Profile

**What it is:** A sharpened definition of the role, grounded in market reality.

**What's included:**
- 🎯 **Role scope** — Key accountabilities and decisions this person owns
- 📊 **Success criteria** — How you'll measure if this hire is working (quantitative and qualitative)
- ⭐ **Non-negotiables vs. nice-to-haves** — What's truly required vs. what's a bonus
- 👤 **Typical background range** — Experience level, background, and skills required
- 🔄 **Success profile** — Behaviors, mindset, and approach that win in your context

**Why this matters:**  
Most hiring briefs are vague ("We need a strong leader"). This is specific and measurable. It guides sourcing, screening, interviews, and onboarding.

**Format:** 1-2 page memo with talking points

---

## 2. Market Map & Talent Signal

**What it is:** Intelligence on where credible talent sits and how to reach them.

**What's included:**
- 📏 **Total addressable talent pool** — Realistic market sizing (is talent scarce or abundant?)
- 🗺️ **Talent concentration** — Where they sit (companies, geographies, industries)
- 👥 **Incumbent analysis** — Who's doing this role well, what makes them successful
- 🎯 **Sourcing channels** — How to find and reach candidates (direct outreach, recruiters, networks)
- 📋 **Shortlist shape** — Realistic number of candidates at each stage, conversion rates
- ⏱️ **Time-to-hire** — Expected search timeline based on market conditions

**Why this matters:**  
It tells you if you're searching in the right places, how many candidates you should expect to see, and how long this will realistically take.

**Format:** Visual map + narrative analysis (2-3 pages)

---

## 3. Compensation & Market Competitiveness

**What it is:** Directional guidance on salary, bonus, and equity expectations.

**What's included:**
- 💵 **Base salary range** — 25th, 50th, and 75th percentiles for this role in your market
- 📈 **Bonus expectations** — Typical bonus structure and percentage
- 📊 **Equity positioning** — Stock options or equity grants (if startup) vs. competitors
- 💼 **Total compensation** — How your package stacks up against market
- 🎯 **Competitive positioning** — Whether you're positioning above, at, or below market
- ⚠️ **Risk flags** — If your band is unrealistic, we'll flag it

**Why this matters:**  
It ensures you're in the market to attract talent. Offer $150K when the market pays $250K? You won't get qualified candidates.

**Format:** Summary table + narrative (1 page)

---

## 4. Risk Flags & Search Strategy

**What it is:** Intelligence on where this search will be hard and how to mitigate.

**What's included:**
- 🚩 **Hard spots** — Where you'll struggle (talent scarcity, competition, cultural fit, etc.)
- 💡 **Mitigation strategies** — How to overcome them
- 📋 **Recommended process** — Interview approach, assessment tools, decision criteria
- 🤝 **Cultural fit** — What to look for, what to watch out for
- ⏳ **Onboarding plan** — How to set this person up for success in first 90 days
- 📞 **Sourcing recommendations** — Specific channels, companies, networks to target

**Why this matters:**  
It turns insights into action. You don't just know where the risks are — you know how to navigate them.

**Format:** Narrative + checklist (2-3 pages)

## What to Do With Them

### If you're running the search internally:
1. Use the **Role Clarity** to brief your hiring manager and recruiting team
2. Use the **Market Map** to guide sourcing (which companies, channels, networks)
3. Use the **Compensation** data to set your offer level
4. Use the **Risk & Strategy** to run an effective interview process

### If you're hiring an executive recruiter:
1. Share all deliverables with your recruiter
2. Use them to hold your recruiter accountable to the brief
3. Use them as criteria for evaluating candidates the recruiter sends

### If you want our help:
1. We can source candidates directly using the market map
2. We can vet candidates using the role clarity and success profile
3. We can coach you on offers using the compensation data
4. We can guide onboarding using the 90-day plan
