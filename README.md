See the leadership market clearly, before you commit to a search.

Most executive hires go wrong before the first candidate is approached. A misaligned brief, an unrealistic salary band, or an underestimated talent pool turns a 90-day search into a six-month drain. The Leadership MRI Scan fixes that at the front end.


What is it?

A same-day intelligence diagnostic for founders, boards, and investors who have a mission-critical leadership role on their mind.

Instead of launching straight into a full search, we run an MRI of the role and the market — giving you an objective, data-led view before you commit time, budget, or internal attention.

Delivered within hours, not weeks. Powered by AI-enabled market mapping.


What you receive


Role clarity and success profile — a sharpened definition of the role, outcomes, and non-negotiables grounded in market reality
Market map and talent signal — a real view of the talent pool, where credible candidates sit today, and shortlist shape
Compensation and market competitiveness — directional read on base, bonus, and equity expectations
Risk flags and search strategy — where this search is likely to be hard, and recommended options for timing and process design



How it works


Briefing call (20–30 minutes) — we unpack the role, context, and success criteria together
Intelligence phase (same afternoon) — AI-enabled market mapping combining Claude, Perplexity, and other proprietary tools and databases
Read-out and recommendations (20–30 minutes) — findings walkthrough, often the same day



Indicative applications


Hiring a Chief of Staff, Chief Revenue Officer, or Head of Growth
Investor pressure or board scrutiny requiring a defensible, data-led view
Entering a new market or sector where your internal talent map is thin
A role that has stalled, dragged, or misfired before
